HOW TO PLAY
THE COMPLETE SK RUSH GUIDE

Your UFO flies forward on its own, forever accelerating. Your only job is to steer it clear of danger, scoop up everything glowing, and survive as deep into the void as you can.

🕹️ CONTROLS
🕹️

MOVE — JOYSTICK

Use the on-screen stick at bottom-left to steer. It floats near your thumb, not on the ship — so you always keep a clear view of your UFO.

⌨️

MOVE — KEYBOARD

On desktop, steer with ARROW KEYS or WASD instead of the joystick. Both work everywhere; use whichever feels natural.

⚡

BOOST

Hold the BOOST button (bottom-right) or SPACE / SHIFT to surge forward. Boosting burns your energy meter, so use it wisely.

💚

SHIELDS

You start with 3 shield hearts, shown top-left. Every hazard hit costs one heart and grants a brief flash of invincibility.

☄️ HAZARDS — AVOID THESE
Asteroid / Debris — solid, tumbling rock
Comet — fast diagonal streaker
Laser Gate — fly through the hole in the ring
Alien Ship — weaves side to side
Black Hole — pulls you in; dodge the core
💎 PICK-UPS — GRAB THESE
Energy Ring — score + refills boost
Gem — rare, big bonus score
Magnet — auto-pulls nearby rings & gems to you for a few seconds
Shield Orb — grants temporary full invincibility
🏆 SCORING & COMBOS
📏

DISTANCE

Every metre you survive adds straight to your score — just staying alive keeps the number climbing.

🔥

COMBO

Collecting rings/gems back-to-back builds a combo multiplier. Getting hit resets it — chain your pick-ups!

😅

CLOSE CALLS

Skim past a hazard without hitting it and you'll bank a "Close Call" bonus. Flying brave pays off.

🚩

MILESTONES

Every 250m earns a bonus and a celebration banner. The zones around you also shift colour as you go deeper.

💡 PRO TIPS
  • Save boost for open stretches with lots of energy rings — that's how you build big combos fast.
  • Near a black hole? Steer hard against the pull early; fighting it late is much harder.
  • Laser gates only hurt the ring itself — the hole in the middle is always safe.
  • A shield orb is your best moment to boost recklessly through a dense hazard field.
